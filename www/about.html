Ithaca's annual Fall Apple Harvest Festival is a celebration of Ithaca's culture and Fall harvest. A way to honor farmers, local shops and vendors, and beautiful Ithaca, NY, this event attracts hundreds of visitors from all over the world during its run. For 36 years, the Harvest festival has been held in Downtown Ithaca as a way for visitors and Ithacans to explore the gems of Upstate New York. Hand-made products, shops, and fresh produce are just some characteristics of this event!

All Things Apple

Source

This year, our sponsors, The Tompkins Trust Company, The Marriott Downtown Ithaca, Ithaca Times, and Finger Lakes Cider Week, bring you apples in all forms. From cider to donuts to warm pies and caramel apples, your apple cravings will be satisfied!

Vendors

Selling a plethora of handmade items or items produced from local ingredients, upstate New York's vendors share their talents with you! Knit scarves, fine jewellery, essential oils, and spicy maple butter are some vendor specialties.
