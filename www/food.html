With thousands of Cornell University and Ithaca College students brining unique cultures and diverse backgrounds to the city, Ithaca's food scene is globally inspired. The Apple Harvest Festival's many food vendors reflect this inspiration through fusions of Latin, Asian, Mediterranean, and American foods. To foodies everywhere - don't miss out on the opportunity to try these different cusines. Come with an empty stomach and an open heart!
