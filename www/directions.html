When: Sep. 28 - Sep 30, 10 am - 8pm
Where: Downtown Ithaca, NY, 14853

The following map shows the Commons in red pin. Located on W State St and nestled in between E Seneca and E Green St, the Commons is in the heart of town. For visitors staying at the Hilton Garden Inn or the Mariott Downtown, the Commons is a convenient two-minute walk away.

Driving

If you choose to drive to Ithaca Commons, parking is available in the conveniently located Green, Seneca, and Cayuga Street garages at flat charge of $5 per day. No re-entry is permitted.

Green St. Garage | Source
Green Street Garage: 122 E Green St, Ithaca, NY 14850
Seneca Street Garage: 215 N Tioga St, Ithaca, NY 14850
Cayuga Street Garage: 301 Cayuga St, Ithaca, NY 14850
For more information, visit the Park Ithaca Website.

Public Transporation

A Tcat Bus | Source

If you prefer not to take your car and walk instead, several TCAT public transportation buses go to Downtown Ithaca. Many of these bus routes run as frequently as every fifteen minutes. Routes 10, 15, 30, 45, 70, and 72 are among some of the numerous buses that run through Downtown all day. Explore exact details about routes on the TCAT website.
